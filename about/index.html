About me

My name is Bastien Girardet, I’m orginially from Lausanne and I do live in St. Gallen. I am majoring in Economics with a minor in Data Science at the University of St. Gallen.
I’am passionate about both theorical and practical computer science, programming, data science, entrepreneurship and self-development. Since I was little I always had few projects I was working on (Electronics / Programming / Sciences). I always loved trying things out. It might explains why today entrepreneurship is so appealing to me. Today I apply my skills to projects I believe in. Many of them are machine learning related (NLP, sentiment analysis, Naïve Bayes, Neural network, etc…).

I believe that today, basic programming experience should be mandatory. Technology grows at an unprecedent pace and world is changing faster than decades ago. Digitalization is slowly but surely transforming the world into binary digits. In the near future today’s jobs will be replaced by algorithms run by machines so I think that now it is crucial time for us to understand the power of technology and the challenges that it will generate in the world to come.
